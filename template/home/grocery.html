Essential Grocery Assistance

Distributing Grocery Kits to Families in Need

Our Essential Grocery Assistance program is dedicated to ensuring that families in need have access to basic food supplies during challenging times. Through this initiative, we distribute grocery kits containing staple items and essential supplies to help families maintain a healthy and balanced diet.

We work closely with local community organizations, donors, and volunteers to identify those who require support. This program has proven invaluable during economic downturns and unexpected crises, ensuring that no family is left without the necessary resources to thrive.

At AKG Charitable Society, Kakkodi, Kozhikode, we remain committed to expanding our outreach and sustaining this initiative. Your support is crucial in helping us serve even more families in need.
