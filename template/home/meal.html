Hospital Without Hunger

Free Food to Patients Programme

We launched “Hospital Without Hunger” in April 2011 by providing free food to 20 people per day at a Government Hospital in Kozhikode. As the need grew, we forged strong partnerships with local caterers and community volunteers to deliver free, nutritious meals directly to patients and their bystanders.

As of March 31, 2022, we have served 850,000 meals. Our service is available to anyone in need. Most patients come from distant areas and belong to economically and socially backward sections of society. The free food initiative has received overwhelming support from social organizations and philanthropic individuals.

AKG Charitable Society, Kakkodi, Kozhikode remains dedicated to uplifting lives and ensuring that every meal makes a difference.
