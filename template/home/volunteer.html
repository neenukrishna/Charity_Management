Volunteer with Us

Your compassion can change lives

Make a Difference

At AKG Charitable Society, Kakkodi, Kozhikode, we believe that every act of kindness creates a ripple of hope. Volunteering with us means joining a community dedicated to uplifting those in need through compassionate service.

Whether you can spare a few hours a week or commit to long-term service, your contribution is invaluable. We provide training and support to empower you as a vital part of our team, ensuring you make a lasting impact.

Your skills, time, and passion can help us extend our services to more individuals in crisis, offering them hope and a chance for a better tomorrow.

Join us and be the change you wish to see in the world!
