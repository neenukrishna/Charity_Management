About Us

AKG Charitable Society, Kakkodi, Kozhikode is a dedicated non-profit organization committed to uplifting communities through social welfare initiatives. Our mission is to provide humanitarian aid, support the underprivileged, and foster a compassionate society. We focus on healthcare, education, emergency relief, palliative care, and community development, ensuring that every individual receives the support they need. Through volunteer efforts, donation drives, and public engagement, we aim to create a meaningful impact in the lives of those in need.
